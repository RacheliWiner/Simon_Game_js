How to play the simon game?



Simon is an electronic memory game that, in addition to being fun, also improves visual memory.
At the beginning Simon lights up one of the buttons, and your job is to press that button (which lights up when you press it).
Next time the game will light up the button that lit up last time, and add another button that will light up after it.

there are six colors: 
red, blue, green,
yellow, orange and purple
